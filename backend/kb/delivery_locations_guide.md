# Delivery Locations Guide

**Last Updated:** 2026-03-10
**Applies To:** All AcmeCo Industrial accounts

---

## Overview

Each AcmeCo account can have one or more approved delivery locations. Orders are fulfilled to a specific delivery location on the account. New locations must be approved before orders can be placed to them.

---

## What Is a Delivery Location?

A delivery location is a physical site where AcmeCo delivers industrial gases. Each location has:
- A **Location Name** (e.g., "Oak Park Warehouse")
- A **Location Code** (e.g., "OAK-01") — a short unique identifier used on orders and invoices
- A **Full Delivery Address** including unit/dock number where applicable
- A **Site Contact** — name and phone number of the person who receives deliveries

---

## What Is a Location Code?

A location code is a short alphanumeric identifier (e.g., "CHI-02", "DET-01") that AcmeCo assigns when a delivery location is approved. It appears on:
- Order confirmations
- Invoices
- Delivery notes and PODs

Location codes are used internally for routing and logistics. If your order confirmation or invoice shows a location code you don't recognise, contact your Account Manager — it may indicate the wrong location was selected at order time.

---

## How to Add a New Delivery Location

### Step 1 — Contact Your Account Manager

Email or call your Account Manager to request a new delivery location. Provide:
- Full delivery address (street, city, state, ZIP)
- Site contact name and direct phone number
- Preferred delivery window (e.g., Mon–Fri, 7am–3pm)
- Any site access instructions (gate code, dock bay number, hazmat storage area)

### Step 2 — Account Manager Submits for Approval

Your Account Manager will submit the location to AcmeCo's logistics team for review. Approval criteria include:
- Location is within AcmeCo's service area
- Site can safely receive and store industrial gas cylinders
- All required contact information is provided

### Step 3 — Approval and Location Code Assignment

- Standard approval SLA: **3–5 business days**
- Rush requests (for urgent operational needs): contact your Account Manager — may be approved in 1–2 business days subject to logistics availability
- Once approved, AcmeCo will email you the assigned Location Code
- The new location will be available for selection at next order placement

| Delivery hours | Yes | Hours when the site accepts deliveries |
| Site access instructions | Recommended | Gate codes, hazmat area, dock bay |
| Special handling requirements | If applicable | e.g., refrigerated storage, limited vehicle access |

---

## Modifying an Existing Location

To update an existing delivery location (e.g., new site contact, updated address):
- Email your Account Manager with the Location Code and the changes needed
- Minor updates (contact name/number) are applied within **1 business day**
- Address changes require re-approval through the standard 3–5 day process

---

## Removing a Delivery Location

To deactivate a delivery location:
- Email your Account Manager with the Location Code and reason for removal
- Deactivation is effective within 1 business day
- Any in-progress or scheduled orders to that location will be flagged for your review before deactivation completes

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I add a new delivery site to my account?**
A: Contact your Account Manager with the full address, site contact, and delivery hours. Standard approval takes 3–5 business days.

**Q: What is the location code on my invoice?**
A: It's a short identifier (e.g., OAK-01) that AcmeCo assigns to each approved delivery site. It's used for routing and appears on your invoices and delivery notes.

**Q: I need a new location approved urgently — can it be done faster?**
A: Rush approvals are possible in 1–2 business days. Contact your Account Manager directly and explain the urgency.

**Q: Can I place an order to an unapproved address?**
A: No. Orders can only be placed to approved delivery locations on your account. This ensures safe delivery and compliance with hazmat transport regulations.

**Q: How many delivery locations can my account have?**
A: There is no hard limit. Each location must be individually approved.
