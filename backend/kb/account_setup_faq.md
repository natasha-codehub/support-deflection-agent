# Account Setup & Management FAQ

**Last Updated:** 2026-04-01
**Applies To:** All AcmeCo Industrial accounts

---

## Overview

This document covers common questions about managing your AcmeCo account — including account manager contacts, adding users, updating billing information, and understanding payment terms.

---

## Account Manager

Every AcmeCo account is assigned a dedicated Account Manager who is your primary point of contact for:
- Order queries and escalations
- New delivery location approvals
- Contract and pricing discussions
- Account configuration changes

**To find out who your Account Manager is:**
- Check the footer of any AcmeCo invoice — your AM's name and email are listed
- Email **accounts@acmeco.com** with your Account ID (format: ACC-XXXX)
- Call AcmeCo main support at 1-800-ACMECO-1 and provide your Account ID

If your Account Manager has changed and you haven't been notified, email accounts@acmeco.com to request an introduction to your new AM.

---

## Adding Users to Your Account

Authorised contacts on your account can place orders, request PODs, and raise disputes.

### To add a new authorised contact:
1. Email your Account Manager or **accounts@acmeco.com**
2. Include: Account ID, new contact's full name, email address, job title, and access level requested (order placement, billing only, full access)
3. New users are added within **2 business days**
4. The new contact will receive a welcome email from AcmeCo with account access details

### Access Levels

| Access Level | Can Place Orders | Can View Invoices | Can Raise Disputes | Can Request PODs |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Full Access | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Order Only | Yes | No | No | Yes |
| Billing Only | No | Yes | Yes | No |
| Read Only | No | Yes | No | Yes |

## Updating Billing Contact or Email

Your billing contact receives all invoices, credit notes, and payment reminders.

To update your billing contact or billing email address:
- Email your Account Manager or **ar@acmeco.com**
- Subject line: `Billing Contact Update – [Account ID]`
- Include: Account ID, current billing email, new billing email (and new contact name if applicable)
- Changes are applied within **1 business day**

---

## Payment Terms

Standard payment terms vary by account and are agreed at contract time:
- **Net 30** — payment due 30 days from invoice date
- **Net 45** — payment due 45 days from invoice date
- **Net 60** — payment due 60 days from invoice date

Your payment terms appear on every invoice. To request a change in payment terms, contact your Account Manager. Changes require credit review and typically take 5–7 business days to process.

**Late payment:** Invoices unpaid beyond terms incur a 1.5% monthly late fee. AcmeCo will notify you by email before applying late fees.

## Understanding Your Account ID

Your Account ID (format: ACC-XXXX) is your unique identifier across all AcmeCo systems. It appears on:
- All invoices and credit notes
- Order confirmations
- Any support communications

You will need your Account ID when contacting support, raising disputes, or submitting tax exemption certificates.

## Updating Company Information

To update your registered company name, primary address, or VAT/tax registration number:
- Email **accounts@acmeco.com** with supporting documentation (e.g., updated company registration, rebranding letter)
- Processing time: **3–5 business days**

---

## Account Suspension and Credit Holds

Accounts may be placed on a **credit hold** if invoices are significantly overdue. On credit hold:
- New orders cannot be placed
- Existing scheduled orders may be paused
- Contact **ar@acmeco.com** immediately to resolve outstanding balances and lift the hold

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Who is my account manager?**
A: Your Account Manager's name and email appear on your invoices. You can also email accounts@acmeco.com with your Account ID to get their contact details.

**Q: How do I add a new user to my account?**
A: Email your Account Manager or accounts@acmeco.com with the new user's name, email, job title, and required access level. They'll be added within 2 business days.

**Q: How do I update my billing email address?**
A: Email ar@acmeco.com with subject "Billing Contact Update – [Account ID]", including your current and new billing email. Changes apply within 1 business day.

**Q: What are my payment terms?**
A: Your payment terms (Net 30, Net 45, or Net 60) appear on every invoice. Contact your Account Manager to discuss a change.

**Q: I've changed company names — how do I update my account?**
A: Email accounts@acmeco.com with supporting documentation. Updates take 3–5 business days.

**Q: My account is on hold — what do I do?**
A: Contact ar@acmeco.com immediately to discuss outstanding balances and arrange a payment to lift the hold.
