# Proof of Delivery (POD) Retrieval Guide

**Last Updated:** 2026-01-20
**Applies To:** All AcmeCo Industrial accounts

---

## Overview

A Proof of Delivery (POD) is a document that confirms an order was delivered to your site. It is generated automatically upon successful delivery and is available as a PDF download. PODs are commonly required for accounts payable reconciliation, dispute resolution, and audit purposes.

---

## What Is a POD?

A POD document includes:
- **Order ID** and **Invoice Number**
- **Delivery date and time**
- **Delivery location** (address and location code)
- **Items delivered** (SKU, description, quantity)
- **Driver name**
- **Signature** of the person who received the delivery at your site
- **GPS coordinates** of the delivery point (where available)

PODs are generated in PDF format. They cannot be edited or reissued in a modified form.

---

## How to Retrieve a POD

### Option 1 — Request via Support

Email **pods@acmeco.com** with:
- Subject: `POD Request – [Order ID or Invoice Number]`
- Your Account ID
- The Order ID (ORD-XXXX) or Invoice Number (INV-XXXX)
- Delivery location (if you have multiple sites)

AcmeCo will respond with the POD PDF within **1 business day**.

### Option 2 — Ask Your Account Manager

Your Account Manager can retrieve and forward the POD directly. This is the fastest option for urgent requests.

### Option 3 — Customer Portal (Where Available)

If your account has access to the AcmeCo customer portal, PODs are available under **Orders → Delivered → [Order ID] → View POD**. Portal access is not available to all accounts — contact your Account Manager to request access.

---

## POD Availability

- PODs are generated automatically when the driver marks a delivery complete in AcmeCo's route management system
- PODs for deliveries made after **1 January 2022** are available digitally
- For deliveries before January 2022, PODs exist as scanned paper documents and may take up to **3 business days** to retrieve
- PODs are available for **7 years** from the date of delivery, in line with AcmeCo's document retention policy

---

## POD Format

- All PODs are issued as **PDF documents**
- File naming convention: `POD_[OrderID]_[DeliveryDate].pdf` (e.g., `POD_ORD-4522_2026-05-20.pdf`)
- PODs are not available in Excel, Word, or image formats
- If you need data from multiple PODs in bulk (e.g., for a quarterly audit), email pods@acmeco.com with date range and account ID — a bulk export can be arranged

---

## Who Can Request a POD?

PODs can be requested by:
- Any registered contact on the account (including AP contacts and site managers)
- Your designated Account Manager on your behalf

PODs are account-confidential and will only be shared with verified account contacts.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I get the POD for my last delivery?**
A: Email pods@acmeco.com with your Order ID or Invoice Number and Account ID. You'll receive the PDF within 1 business day.

**Q: Was my order actually delivered? The system shows "In Transit."**
A: If the status shows "In Transit," delivery has not yet been confirmed in our system. PODs are only generated upon confirmed delivery. Check back after the ETA date or contact your Account Manager for a live status update.

**Q: How long does AcmeCo keep POD records?**
A: PODs are retained for 7 years from the date of delivery.

**Q: My POD shows the wrong quantity delivered. What do I do?**
A: If the POD quantity differs from what you actually received, raise a short pay dispute via disputes@acmeco.com (see the Short Pay Dispute Process document for full instructions).

**Q: Can I get PODs for all my deliveries in the last 6 months?**
A: Yes. Email pods@acmeco.com with your Account ID and the date range. A bulk export will be arranged — allow 2–3 business days for large requests.

**Q: The POD shows delivery was signed by someone I don't recognise. Is that a problem?**
A: It may indicate the delivery was signed by a temporary worker or contractor at your site. If you cannot verify the signatory, contact your Account Manager to review the delivery record.
